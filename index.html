HERE ARE THE RULES OF THE GAME

  1. Each player gets a turn to play the game
  2. The player rolls the dice by clicking the roll button
  3. If he rolls any number between 2 and 6, he gets point equivalent
    to the number rolled
  4. If he rolls 1, he looses all his former points and his turn ends
  5. If the player decides to hold, he keeps his points but his turn ends
  6. The winner is the one that gets to 100 points first
  7. Goodluck!!!!!!!!!!
